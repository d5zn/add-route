Support and Privacy Information

About addicted

addicted is a creative solutions laboratory that specializes in innovative business and community applications. Our club route application helps clubs and communities create beautiful visual representations of their routes and activities.

This application uses Strava API to access your workout data and create personalized visualizations.

How It Works

1. Connect to Strava

Authorize addicted to access your Strava data securely through OAuth.

2. Select Your Workout

Choose from your recent cycling activities to create a visualization.

3. Customize Your Design

Adjust metrics, colors, and layout to match your preferences.

4. Download or Share

Save your workout visualization as an image or share it on social media.

Frequently Asked Questions

What data does addicted access?

We access your basic activity data (distance, time, elevation, speed) and route information to create workout visualizations.

Is my data safe?

Yes, we use secure OAuth authentication and only process data temporarily for visualization purposes.

Can I revoke access?

Absolutely. You can revoke addicted's access to your Strava data at any time in your Strava account settings.

Does this cost anything?

addicted is free to use. We may offer premium features in the future.

Data Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously:

  • We only access workout data necessary for visualization
  • Your data is processed securely and not stored permanently
  • We comply with Strava's API terms of service
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Strava settings

Troubleshooting

Connection Issues

If you're having trouble connecting to Strava:

  • Make sure you're logged into Strava in your browser
  • Check that you have cycling activities in your Strava account
  • Try refreshing the page and reconnecting

Visualization Problems

If your workout isn't displaying correctly:

  • Ensure your activity has GPS data (route information)
  • Try selecting a different workout
  • Check that your activity is set to public or "Followers" visibility

Privacy Policy

Last updated:

At addicted, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Strava Data

When you connect your Strava account, we access the following information:

  • Basic activity data (distance, time, elevation, speed)
  • Route information (GPS coordinates, polyline data)
  • Activity metadata (name, date, type)

Account Information

We may store minimal account information necessary for authentication:

  • Strava athlete ID
  • Encrypted access and refresh tokens
  • Connection timestamps

Usage & Analytics Data

To understand how the product is used and to prevent abuse we collect limited analytics data:

  • Session identifiers generated by the app
  • Club selection (e.g. NOT IN PARIS, HEDONISM) associated with downloads
  • Download events (format, timestamp, related club)
  • Unique Strava connection events per day
  • Visit information (page path, date, time)
  • Technical metadata such as IP address and user agent sent by your browser

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Creating workout visualizations
  • Providing and maintaining the core functionality of our service
  • Maintaining your account connection with Strava
  • Measuring product usage (unique connections, downloads, visits)
  • Diagnosing technical issues and preventing fraudulent or abusive behaviour

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Data Processing

Your workout data is processed temporarily to generate visualizations. We do not permanently store your activity routes or detailed GPS data on our servers.

Analytics Storage

Analytics and connection metadata (session IDs, club selections, download/visit logs, IP addresses, user agents) are stored in our managed PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway. These records help us produce aggregate statistics and maintain service reliability.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Encrypted access tokens
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Role-based access to production infrastructure

Data Retention & Deletion

Analytics and connection metadata are retained for as long as needed to provide aggregate reporting and ensure platform security. You can request removal of your analytics records or account information at any time by contacting support@addicted.design. We will delete or anonymize your data unless we are required to retain it for legal or operational reasons.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Revoke access to your Strava account at any time
  • Request deletion of your account information
  • Opt out of data collection by disconnecting your account

To revoke access, go to your Strava account settings and remove addicted from your connected applications.

Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with Strava API. Please review Strava's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your data: Strava Privacy Policy

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and local storage to keep you signed in, remember your club selection, and maintain session analytics IDs. We do not use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us
